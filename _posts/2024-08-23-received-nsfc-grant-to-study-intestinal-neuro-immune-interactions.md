I am thrilled to announce that our lab has been awarded an NSFC General Program grant to investigate a newly identified subset of muscularis macrophages / microglia-like cells. These cells modulate motor neuron plasticity and function, forming a critical link between intestinal inflammation and impaired gut motility.
